Blender Addon

I created an addon in Blender called Breakdown-fy, which automates animating the objects falling down seen a lot in many movie / animation behind the scene videos. I created this addon after wanting to recreate this effect, but finding out that the only way to achieve this is to do it by hand. it is held on github, and can be downloaded for free.
